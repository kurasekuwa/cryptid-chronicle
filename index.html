Mysterious Lake Creature Spotted at 2:13 AM

Reported by Saj Al Karma | February 1, 2026

Campers at Blackridge Lake reported a massive shape moving beneath the surface just after 2 AM. Witnesses described glowing ripples and a low, echoing sound that "didn't come from any animal they'd ever heard before."

One camper claims the water "rose up like something enormous turned over beneath it." No official explanation has been provided.

Local historians point out that similar stories date back over 80 years, describing a "deep-water watcher" said to surface only on the coldest nights.

Authorities have closed the lake after dark, though they insist this is "purely precautionary."
